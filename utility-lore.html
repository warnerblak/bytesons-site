Utility & Lore

A plain-language frame for reading Bytesons.

Bytesons is an Ethereum-native NFT collection of on-chain characters designed for long-term persistence.

This page explains the project without metaphors. The goal is clarity.

How to use this page
  • New here — read this first, then browse the collection.
  • Checking claims — use the contract as the source of truth.
  • Writing copy — use Signal Audit to pressure-test language.
Reference: Contract, Index, Archive
Mechanism

Time in Bytesons is expressed through immutability: core traits and metadata are fixed on Ethereum and do not change.

If the work is readable later, the system did its job.

Principles
  • Permanence — prioritize durability over novelty.
  • Restraint — fewer promises, clearer signals.
  • Readability — keep the collection legible to a first-time viewer.
What it is
  • Artifacts — characters with a consistent visual language.
  • Index — a stable place to link and reference the work.
  • Tools — small utilities that earn their presence (holder-only).
What it isn’t
  • Not a promise of outcomes.
  • Not a hype engine.
  • Not dependent on constant updates to remain meaningful.
