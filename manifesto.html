Manifesto

A constraint set.

Bytesons is an Ethereum-native NFT collection of on-chain characters.

This is not a roadmap. It’s a constraint set: choices that reduce noise and make the work readable over time.

Time in Bytesons is expressed through immutability: core traits and metadata are fixed on Ethereum. Bytesons is not an evolving-state NFT.

Principles
  • Quiet by design.
  • Minimal systems. Verifiable references.
  • Fixed artifacts over changing narratives.
  • Structure that survives attention cycles.
  • Tools exist to improve readability, not to manufacture demand.

Ownership is the ability to hold a fixed artifact as the system around it changes.
