Signal Audit

A holder-only review of language, structure, and intent.

This tool evaluates signal — not hype, not price, not outcomes. It’s designed to help you see how your project reads to someone encountering it for the first time. to access, Please connect your Ethereum Wallet to verify your Bytesons NFT holdings.

Notes
  • This tool does not evaluate market performance, pricing, or future outcomes.
  • It does not replace taste or judgment.
  • It is most useful early — before language hardens into habit.
Audit Input

Paste the text you want reviewed. This can include a bio, homepage copy, project description, or any combination of the above.

The audit focuses on clarity, coherence, originality, restraint, and trust. Financial language and speculation are intentionally ignored.
