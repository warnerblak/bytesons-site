Bytesons

Ethereum-native characters built for permanence.

Bytesons is an Ethereum-native NFT collection of on-chain characters.

Ownership is the ability to hold a fixed artifact as the system around it changes.

Explore the visual system → Palette Study

The work is quiet by design: early internet aesthetics, minimal systems, and structure that can be read years later.

Mechanism

Time in Bytesons is expressed through immutability: core traits and metadata are fixed on Ethereum. Bytesons is not an evolving-state NFT.

“Permanence” here describes what is fixed on-chain. It does not promise outcomes.
